How to Play

🎬 The Goal

Read the twisted (but technically accurate) plot summary and guess the movie title. Use the on-screen keyboard to reveal letters one at a time, or go straight for the full solve!

💰 Scoring

Each movie starts at 500 points. Wrong consonant guesses cost 30 points — vowels are always free. Solve early with more letters still hidden for bonus multipliers: 1.5× with 80%+ hidden, 1.25× with 50%+ hidden.

❌ Strikes

You have 3 strikes for the entire game. Wrong full-solve attempts cost a strike. Run out and it's game over.

⌨️ Physical Keyboard

On desktop, just type letters directly — no clicking required. Press Enter to open the solve modal, and Escape to dismiss it.

📅 Daily Challenge

Same 5 movies for everyone every day, one guess per movie, no letter hints. Compare your score with friends!
