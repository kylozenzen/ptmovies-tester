How to Play

🎬 The Goal

Read the twisted (but technically accurate) plot summary and guess the movie title. Use the keyboard to reveal letters one at a time, or go straight for the full solve.

💰 Scoring

Each movie starts at 500 points. Wrong consonant guesses cost 30 points — vowels are always free. Solve early with 80%+ hidden for a 1.5× multiplier, or 50%+ for 1.25×.

❌ Strikes

You have 3 strikes per game. Wrong full-title guesses cost a strike. Run out and the screening ends early.

⌨️ Physical Keyboard

On desktop, type letters directly. Press Enter to open the solve dialog, Escape to dismiss it.

📅 Daily Ritual

Same 5 movies for every player every day. One guess per film. No letter hints. Share your scorecard.
